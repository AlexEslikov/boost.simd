template<typename T>
struct boost::simd::logical< T >

Typed boolean value.

logical<T> is a typed boolean value which can be vectorized in a portable way (contrary to bool). Its semantic is equivalent to bool on all aspect but may provide a compiler dependent, optimized bool-to-type conversion.
