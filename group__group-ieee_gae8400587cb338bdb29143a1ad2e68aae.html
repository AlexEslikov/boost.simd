template<typename T >
auto boost::simd::exponent ( T const &  x)

Returns the exponent of the floating input.

Semantic:
as_integer_t<T> r = exponent(x);

is similar to:

as_integer_t<T> r = ilogb(x);
Note:

The sign \( \pm \) , exponent e and mantissa m of a floating point entry x are related by x = \(\pm m\times 2^e\), with m between one (included) and two (excluded).

For integral type inputs exponent is always 0 and mantissa reduces to identity.

mantissa, frexp, ldexp
Returns
a value of same type as the input
